Our Story

Welcome to Loza's, a family-owned Ethiopian restaurant where passion and culture blend to create an extraordinary dining experience.At Loza's, we proudly carry forward our family's culinary traditions, passed down through generations, to bring you the most authentic and flavorful Ethiopian dishes. Our roots are deeply intertwined with the vibrant flavors, rich history, and warm hospitality of Ethiopia, and we invite you to embark on a culinary journey with us.

Prepare to tantalize your taste buds with our extensive menu of traditional Ethiopian delights. Each dish is meticulously crafted with love and care, using the freshest locally sourced ingredients. From the iconic injera, a soft and tangy sourdough flatbread, to the aromatic wats, slow-cooked stews bursting with robust flavors, and the tantalizingly spiced tibs, every bite will transport you to the bustling streets of Addis Ababa!
